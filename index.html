Commands

.play

Play music from YouTube, Spotify, or SoundCloud

.pause

Pause the currently playing track

.skip

Skip the current song

queue

Show the current music queue

.stop

Stop music and clear the queue

.resume

resume the music

.loop

loop the music

.shuffle

Shuffle the music queue

.jump

jump to specific song in the queue

.autoplay

automatically play songs.

.saveplaylist

save your spotify playlist.

.loadplaylist

load your saved playlist.

.removeplaylist

remove your saved playlist.
